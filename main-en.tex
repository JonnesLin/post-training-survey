\begin{abstract}
This book systematically traces the evolution of post-training for Large Language Models (LLMs) and Vision-Language Models (VLMs) from an evolutionary perspective.
Unlike traditional surveys that organize content by method categories, this book divides the field into seven problem-driven evolutionary threads,
tracing the ``problem $\rightarrow$ solution $\rightarrow$ new problem $\rightarrow$ improved solution'' development chain within each thread,
and revealing the flow of ideas across different research directions through cross-thread references.
The seven threads are:
(1) Instruction Following \& SFT,
(2) Reward Modeling \& Preference Optimization,
(3) Safety \& Alignment,
(4) Reasoning \& Verification,
(5) Multimodal Post-training,
(6) Tool Use \& Agentic Capabilities,
(7) Efficiency \& Data Engineering.
This book provides in-depth analysis of landmark papers within each thread while revealing the overall evolutionary direction of the post-training field through macro-level trend analysis.
\end{abstract}
